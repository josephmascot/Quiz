ABOUT US!

Who are we?

Mr Man USIU is the leading provider of smart and interactive IT solutions in African cities, bringing efficient cashless payment systems to the mostly informal cash-based public transport system in emerging African cities. Mr Man is currently operational in Rwanda, Kigali and Kenya, Nairobi.

Our Vision!

To be the Africa’s customer centric company that creates and designs technology systems that connect people and cities.

Our Mission!

To provide smart IT solutions to Mass urban cities and related service providers in Africa through Reliable, secure and fast systems.
